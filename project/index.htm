Old Man Pokémon Glitch LEGACY


The Old Man Pokémon glitch is where your player name can be used in order to spawn in very specific Pokemon in the original releases of the Red & Blue games. It's well known for finding a ton of Missingno off the side of Cinabar Island, this just tells you what other Pokémon you will find alongside Missingno in these areas. The code is quite poor and needs refining.
