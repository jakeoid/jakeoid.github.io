Game Compatibility.

The following shows the compatiablity of the consoles as to there game. Really and truely its a simple chart and can be used to reference when choosing to purchase a game from a plethora of Online Vendors, or even just your Local Store. A mobile version of this website is avaliable but isn't tested very well.

So how does the following chart apply to you? Look at the cartridge of the game you have purchased or are looking to purchase. Look through the images find the serial code. There's different locations for each of these but for the most part there in the bottom right corner below the 'Official Nintendo Seal' on licensed games.
