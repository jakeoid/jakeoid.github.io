The Text to Image Generator is a resource that was created to serve a very specific purpose, that being the ability to convert a set of text (in a table) to an image for a later resource on Jakeoid. As I worked on it people around me grew interested and as a result it was published with the little to no documentation.

Henceforth in order to create an image in this resource you use a series of #'s and .'s. In order to display a pixel (turn it on) you place a "#". You can turn on and off pixels at will but then when it comes time to skip pixels (leave a box's worth gap) you place a ".". Another thing to be clear with is to make sure your image width & height are correct enough to fit your series of #'s and .'s.
